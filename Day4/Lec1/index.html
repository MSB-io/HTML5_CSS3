Labrador Retriever

Main Information

Breed Name: Labrador Retriever
Country of Origin: Canada
Breeding Time: 19th Century
Type: Companion Dog
Weight: 26 - 40 Kg
Height (Height at the Withers): 55 - 57 cm
Life Span: 10-13 years
Labrador Puppy Price: 200 - 1250 $

Description

The Labrador Retriever is a friendly, outgoing, and high-spirited companion who has more than enough affection to go around. They are well-suited for families and are known for their intelligence and versatility.

Origins

The Labrador Retriever originated in Newfoundland, Canada, where they were bred to assist fishermen in retrieving fish and nets. They were later recognized as a distinct breed in the 19th century.

Temperament

Labrador Retrievers are known for their friendly and outgoing nature. They are highly social dogs that thrive on human interaction. They are also intelligent and eager to please, making them easy to train. They are great with children and other pets, making them an ideal family dog.

Health

Labrador Retrievers are generally healthy dogs, but they can be prone to certain health issues such as hip dysplasia, elbow dysplasia, and obesity. Regular veterinary check-ups and a balanced diet are essential for maintaining their health.

Appearance

Labrador Retrievers are recognized worldwide and are one of the most popular dog breeds. They come in three standard colors: black, yellow, and chocolate. Their appearance may vary slightly based on regional breeding practices.
